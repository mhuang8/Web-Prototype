Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower communities to reduce the environmental impacts of waste in Australia, and contribute to a cleaner, greener world. We motivate and provide opportunities for Australians to clean up, reduce waste, and conserve our precious environment.

How You Can Help

Raise Awareness

Help raise awareness about Clean Up Australia and our cause.
