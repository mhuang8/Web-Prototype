Participate in a Clean-Up

Volunteer by cleaning up!

Our Clean-Up events are a great way for you to volunteer and get involved. You can create or join a Clean-Up event any day of the year, and participate as an individual or a group. If you want to share your Clean-Up photos or experience, tag us using #CleanUpAustralia. Together we can make a difference!

Join our Raffle

Our current ongoing Clean & Green Raffle event provides you the opportunity to win prizes if you participate in any of the Clean-Up events listed on this page.

Create a Clean Up

Take lead and organise your own Clean-Up to make a difference in your community. You can create a private Clean-Up as an individual or with a group, or start a Public Clean-Up Event for members of the community to join in. Creating a Clean-Up is perfect for friends and families who want to help out in local areas in a social, outdoor event.

Join a Clean Up

Signup for a Clean-Up already happening in your community. You can join a registered Clean-Up near you or browse across Australia. Joining an existing Clean-Up is a great opportunity to connect with fellow Australian community members who are passionate about creating change.

Clean Up Australia Day 2021

Mark your calendars, our annual Clean Up Australia day will be held on Sunday, 7 March 2021. We invite everyone to step up and join our largest annual community event, and help make an impact.
