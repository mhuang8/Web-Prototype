Clean & Green
Raffle

Clean up for a chance to win!

The Clean & Green Raffle is the perfect opportunity to get outdoors and do something positive for the environment in a socially responsible way. With no one to do Clean-Ups over the recent months, there has been an increase in rubbish around the neighbourhood. With some friends or family, you can get some physical exercise while cleaning some rubbish around your local area for a chance to win some great waste-free, eco-friendly prizes!

We have partnered with Kappi Australia to bring you a chance to win high quality, re-usable and sustainable products to help you stay green. Join now and gain a chance to win 1 in 5 of their Zero Waste Lifestyle Packs along with a $20 Kappi e-gift card!

How To Enter

  1. Simply participate in a Clean-Up event and take a photo of yourself outside with your cleaned rubbish.
  2. Submit your photo and report your cleanup in the form below to be entered into the raffle!

Help us achieve the goal of 500 participants which to help make a positive impact on the Australian environment. Everyone has the opportunity to be a featured Community Hero, and inspire others to join in!

If you are unable to participate under current circumstances but still wish to join in and contribute, you can opt to make a donation instead. A donation of $25 or more will automatically place you into the raffle for a chance to win! All donations will go towards Clean Up Australia activities, and enable Clean Up Australia to provide educational resources and clean up materials free of charge to schools and youth groups, educating the next generation on the importance of protecting and caring for the environment.
